Audience: The Innocent and the Ambitious

Ursula’s **primary audience** is Ariel, but symbolically it’s anyone who desires something so deeply they’ll ignore the risk. She understands her audience’s weaknesses — innocence, curiosity, and a longing for freedom — and tailors her words accordingly. She becomes what her audience wants to hear: reassuring, witty, and almost trustworthy.
