Purpose: The Temptation of Power

Ursula’s purpose is to **convince Ariel** to make a deal that appears to grant freedom — but really gives Ursula control. Her goal is **manipulation through allure**. She uses flattery, humor, and her “motherly” tone to lower Ariel’s guard. Beneath the charm lies domination. Ursula’s persuasion reflects the danger of language used for selfish gain.
